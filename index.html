«I do not understand the human race. So little love for creatures with a different face. Treating animals like people is no madness or disgrace.»

Honorary Founder: Dr Dolittle

As with any greater than life fictional character, a real-life person inspired the fabulous Dr Dolittle series. That person, all though locally well-known, was generally unassuming, and to this day remains anonymous. But we carry on his work at the location he so carefully selected, uniting as it does, ideal conditions for most animal species to live unconstrained.

This is essential in order to fully apply his precepts regarding meaningful communication with other species, including non-mammalian. Even though a large body of scientific work has by now established their validity, it is only here, at The Animal Farm, that work is carried forward in this respect. As always, the animals are ready, it is us humans who need to be brouht up to speed. Don't hesitate to join us in this great undertaking.
